## Tipografiya

- Font: **Poppins** (Google Fonts)
- H1 Hero: 48–56px, bold, white
- H1 Internal: 40px, semi-bold, dark, centered
- H2 Section: 32px, semi-bold, primary red
- Body: 15–16px, regular, #6B7280, line-height 1.7
- Small Label: 12–14px, uppercase, letter-spacing 1–2px

## Logotip

- **EATURKISH** + bull head silhouette ikonkasi
- Oq fon → qora/qizil versiya
- Qizil fon → oq versiya

## Dekorativ element

- Aylana ichida pichoq ikonkasi, atrofida aylanma matn: **"BITE DELIGHT IN EVERY"**
- Sahifa yon tomonida sticky/absolute

## Header (sticky)

- Fon: `#B91C3C`, balandligi ~70px, padding: 20px 40px
- Chap: EATURKISH logo (oq)
- O'rta: Menu | News | About Us | Contact Us
- O'ng: "Log In" — outline, white border, transparent, rounded-full

## Footer

- Fon: `#B91C3C`, 3-column grid
  1. Contact Us — manzil + 2 telefon
  2. Logo (katta, oq) + italic sitat + social icons (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram)
  3. Working Hours
- Ajratuvchi chiziq + "2022 Pop online. All Rights reserved."
